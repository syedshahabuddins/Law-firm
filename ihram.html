Pillar 1: Ihram

The Sacred State of Purity

Introduction Ihram Tawaf Sa'i & Tahallul

What is Ihram?

Ihram is both the physical garments and the spiritual state you enter. It is a symbol of purity, equality, and submission to Allah.

How to Perform Ihram

Step 1: Purification

Perform Ghusl (full ritual bath) with the intention of Ihram.

Step 2: Prayer

Offer two Rakat prayer like "Dugana Tayatul Wazdu" (the prayer offered after ablution).

Step 3: Ihram Garments

Wear the two pieces of clean, white cloth. For men, this consists of two seamless pieces of cloth. Women wear their regular modest clothing.

Step 4: The Intention (Niyyah)

Declare your intention with sincerity:

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أُرِيدُ الْعُمْرَةَ فَيَسِّرْهَا لِي وَتَقَبَّلْهَا مِنِّي
اے اللہ! میں عمرہ کا ارادہ رکھتا ہوں، اسے میرے لیے آسان فرما اور اسے مجھ سے قبول فرما
Allāhumma innī urīdul-'Umrata fayassirhā lī wa taqabbalhā minnī.
(O Allah, I intend to perform Umrah, so make it easy for me and accept it from me.)

Step 5: The Talbiyah

Recite this frequently from the moment of your intention until you start Tawaf:

لَبَّيْكَ اللَّهُمَّ لَبَّيْكَ، لَبَّيْكَ لَا شَرِيكَ لَكَ لَبَّيْكَ، إِنَّ الْحَمْدَ وَالنِّعْمَةَ لَكَ وَالْمُلْكَ، لَا شَرِيكَ لَكَ
اے اللہ! میں حاضر ہوں، میں حاضر ہوں، میں حاضر ہوں، تیرا کوئی شریک نہیں، میں حاضر ہوں۔ بے شک تمام تعریف اور نعمت تیرے ہی لیے ہے اور بادشاہت تیری ہی ہے، تیرا کوئی شریک نہیں
Labbayka Allāhumma labbayk, labbayka lā sharīka laka labbayk, inna al-ḥamda wa an-ni'mata laka wa al-mulk, lā sharīka lak.
(Here I am, O Allah, here I am. Here I am, You have no partner, here I am. Verily all praise and bounty are Yours, and all sovereignty. You have no partner.)

Restrictions During Ihram

  • No cutting of hair or nails
  • No use of perfumes or scented products
  • No hunting or helping in hunting
  • No sexual relations or flirtatious speech
  • No wearing of stitched clothing (for men)
  • No covering of head (for men)
  • No covering of face (for women)
