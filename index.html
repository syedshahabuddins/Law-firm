About Our Firm

Nissar & Associates is a respected law firm based in Hyderabad, serving clients with a team of highly skilled advocates and legal consultants.

Our firm combines traditional values with modern legal approaches to deliver comprehensive solutions to our clients' legal challenges. We pride ourselves on our deep understanding of Indian law and our commitment to achieving the best possible outcomes for our clients.

Our Practice Areas

Criminal Law

Representation in all criminal matters including bail, appeals, and trials in Sessions Court and High Court.

Family Law

Divorce, child custody, maintenance cases, and other family disputes with compassionate representation.

Property Law

Property disputes, title verification, land acquisition matters, and real estate transactions.

Corporate Law

Company formation, contracts, mergers & acquisitions, and corporate compliance services.

Civil Litigation

All types of civil suits, injunctions, recovery suits, and other civil matters in all courts.

Legal Documentation

Drafting of agreements, wills, affidavits, and other legal documents with precision.

Our Practice Jurisdictions

We practice before the High Court of Telangana (Hyderabad), State Service Tribunal, State Wakf Tribunal, Endowments Tribunals, and State Consumer Forums. We also appear before Trial Courts, Civil Courts, and Criminal Courts across the region.
